THE OG AVENGERS

Iron Man
A genius billionaire, Tony Stark created the Iron Man suit to fight evil. He is a founding me mber of the Avengers and often serves as their technological leader.
Captain America
A World War II super-soldier, Captain America represents the ideal of justice and freedom. His unbreakable shield and leadership make him a key member of the Avengers.
Thor
The god of thunder from Asgard, Thor wields the magical hammer Mjolnir. His immense strength and con trol over lightning make him a powerful force in the Avengers.
Hulk
A brilliant scientist with a temper, Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk when enraged. His incredible strength and in vulnerability are assets in battle, though his temper can be unpredictable.
Black Widow
A former Russian spy, Natasha is a master of hand-to-hand combat and espionage. Her tactical skills and resourcefulness make her a vital member of the team.
Hawkeye
A highly skilled archer with impeccable aim, Hawkeye uses specialized arrows in battle. Despite having no superpowers, he holds his own among the Avengers with his precision and determination.
